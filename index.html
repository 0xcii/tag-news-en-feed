In a statement sent to Bitcoin Magazine, Raph emphasized the importance of collaboration and community engagement in the Ordinals protocol development. Previously funded privately and through personal gifts, this shift ensures the protocol's neutrality while supporting developers. The Ordinals.org website will facilitate community updates and resource sharing related to the Ordinals protocol development. Donations to support the Ordinals core team and the Open Ordinals Institute can be made at Ordinals.org. The organization expects IRS approval for tax-deductible status in 2023, currently accepting Bitcoin donations. The Open Ordinals Institute's establishment assures a promising future for the transformative Bitcoin Ordinals protocol, facilitating its continuous growth and development.
