The project shows how Bitcoin mining can incentivize renewable energy growth by providing producers with additional revenue streams. Other countries are also tapping excess renewables for Bitcoin mining, like El Salvador, which uses its geothermal energy. This dispels the myth that Bitcoin is environmentally hazardous, as much mining uses energy that would otherwise be wasted.  More and more companies and countries are figuring out how to turn waste and surplus energy into the hardest money on earth.
