The acquisition comes at a time when digital currencies are gaining traction, largely due to recent decisions by the US Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) that have eased regulations surrounding cryptocurrencies. This shift has encouraged major companies, including PayPal, to embrace digital currencies. PayPal launched its own US dollar stablecoin last year. International Transactions Although the details about the timeline for the acquisition were not disclosed, the move could position Stripe to compete effectively in the digital finance space.
