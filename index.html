Parish also suggested that the jail terms handed to the duo should highlight to prospective investors the importance of doing research before committing to investing. Meanwhile, the statement also revealed that Jay and Freckleton had paid themselves salaries amounting to more than $178,000. They also withdrew more than $186,000 from Digi Ex’s bank account.
