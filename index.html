He highlighted increasing demand for self-custody after the FTX collapse. Lemke also touched on upcoming US crypto regulations and Getty's roadmap to simplify staking and other complex processes. Stacy Elliott interviewed him at Messari Mainnet 2023.  ( 22 min )
