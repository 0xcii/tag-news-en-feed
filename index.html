In addition to the physical bitcoin, the text of the Genesis Block, the first block of Bitcoin ever mined, will be carried to the Moon as a homage to Satoshi's protocol. The Genesis Block will be displayed on a metal plate with raw hex data and a hologram of the Bitcoin Magazine logo. A replica of the coin and Genesis Block metal plate, placed on a "moon rock" will be auctioned in partnership with Bitcoin Magazine at Bitcoin 2023.
