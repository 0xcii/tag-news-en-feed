"Our expansion of facilities in the Nordics is a testament to our unwavering commitment to utilizing green energy sources," said Abdumalik Mirakhmedov, Executive President and Co-Founder of GDA. "We aim to lead the bitcoin mining industry by setting an example for other players by minimizing our environmental impact."

The first site, located in the southeast US, channels the electricity generated from landfill gas into the local utility grid. Methane, a potent greenhouse gas, is known to be 25 times more adept at trapping heat than carbon dioxide. The company's approach involves combusting methane gas within generators, a strategy that not only mitigates methane but also cuts down on carbon emissions when compared to traditional flaring or venting practices.
